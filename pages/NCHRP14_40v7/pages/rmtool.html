[4] Will larger/taller vegetation negatively impact safety/performance of a median barrier?

[5] Will larger/taller vegetation create sight distance and/or conflicts at access points in the urban/suburban context for vehicles, bicycles, pedestrians, etc.?

[11] Are there cooperative agreements in place for other uses of the ROW such as hay and timber harvesting that will be adversely impacted by managed succession?

If 'Yes,' is this:

[12] If agreements are in place, will local agency resolution/cooperative agreement be able to maintain the site with larger/taller vegetation using their personnel, equipment, etc.?

If 'No,' is this:

[13] Is this corridor and/or adjacent properties being managed for pollinators and other wildlife habitat conservation and/or habitat establishment that may be adversely affected by managed succession?

[14] Can the types of vegetation requirements for pollinators/wildlife be reasonably maintained through managed succession?
